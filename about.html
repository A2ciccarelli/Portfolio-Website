About Me

Hi, I'm Adrianna, a New York area-based instructional designer, problem-solver, and critical thinker. I started my career as a educator 3 years ago and fell in love with instructional design. I decided to pursue my passion for design and development further and received a Master's at the University of Albany in Curriculum Development and Instructional Technology.

My approach blends storytelling, interactivity, and measurable outcomes to craft learning that's engaging, effective and easy to maintain.
